Extrapolation

The call to extrapolate defines what happens if you try to index into the interpolation object with coordinates outside of its bounds in any dimension. The implemented boundary conditions are Throw, Flat, Line, Periodic and Reflect, or you can pass a constant to be used as a "fill" value returned for any out-of-bounds evaluation. Periodic and Reflect require that there is a method of Base.mod that can handle the indices used.

Examples:

itp = interpolate(1:7, BSpline(Linear()))
etpf = extrapolate(itp, Flat())   # gives 1 on the left edge and 7 on the right edge
etp0 = extrapolate(itp, 0)        # gives 0 everywhere outside [1,7]
